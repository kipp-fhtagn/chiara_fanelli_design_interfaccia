This page let you navigate through my Interface design I class.
In the first days, we tried to get confident with interfaces, surfing through the web and focusing on the interfaces with which we were already familiar.

We started a visual analysis of web components.
This helped us to begin a conversation about the bad and good practices and about the UX (User Experience).

In the next days, for the second assignment, we had to revisit a graphic reference, in my case a flyer for an event.

Third thing we had an HTML workshop to play with basic shapes, colors, divs and other weird nerd monsters of brackets.

Last but not least, for our final assignment we had to design a complete website and we had to chose a theme.
